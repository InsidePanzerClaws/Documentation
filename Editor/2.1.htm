Add trash:

Remove trash:
automatically removes additional objects from the basic texture (over the whole playing area).

Fill:
covers the whole playing area with a single selected terrain texture.

Auto:
textures automatically when the elevation is changed. The texture is matched to the terrain shape.

adds additional elements to the basic textures at a selected location.
