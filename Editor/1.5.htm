1.5 Water
 

To flood a low-lying area with water, use the tab "Special", then click on "Water". The longer you left-click on the area, the higher the water level rises. If the water level gets too high, you can lower it by pressing the left mouse button and the SHIFT key simultaneously.

Use this procedure with caution, otherwise too much land could be flooded and that will hinder your own progress.

If you mistakenly flood too much land, use the "Undo Water" button. This reverses the last action. But remember, you can only undo once per operation!

Important!! The water model is extremely realistic, so much so that if you flood a strongly-contoured area (with hills, fissures) then lower the water level, it will run into fissures and low-lying areas to form small lakes!
