2.3 Objects
 

You can select both your side and respective objects to the map from this window.

Special:
Starting points, Production\Assembly points, Markers

Industry and Airfields:
gives access to airfield and industrial options.

Basic Structures:
Neutral mine\factory, German Base, Russian Base, Allied Base, and defensive constructions.

Railroad:
Train, Railroad, Railroad bridge, Allied railway station.

Terrain Objects:
Asphalt, Pavement, Dirty road, Concrete, Bridges, Trees, Walls

"?":
The window in which a player is assigned a conflict party.

Buildings and Details:
Allied buildings, German buildings, Soviet Buildings, City building.

 Locomotive movement mode:

X:
the train remains stationary.

<=> (To the end of railroad and back):
train runs from one end to the other and returns.

>=> (End of the World, looped):
train runs beyond the map, and reappears again on the opposite side.

Allied Units:
Special units, Armored units, Soldiers.

Russian Units:
Special unit, Armed units, Soldiers.

German Units:
Special unit, Armed units, Soldiers.
