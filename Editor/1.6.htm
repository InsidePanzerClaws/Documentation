1.6 Fences and Walls
 

When placing a fence in a field you need to pay attention to the direction buttons (N, S, W, E). These indicate the direction in which you want to build your fence. Each piece of fence has its pivot point in the middle and you can rotate the fence by pressing ENTER key on the numerical keypad. However you must always remember to place the fence (whether on the top, bottom, right or left of the field) parallel to the edge of the field.
