2.2 Altitudes
 

Smooth:
creates a high plateau.

Cone:
lift the terrain up conical.

Random:
"bends" the terrain randomly within the area of the brush.

Flat:
levels the terrain.

Min/Max

Average:
levels the terrain depending from the average value of the surrounding area.

Enlarge:
increases the marked terrain.

Passable:
makes the terrain passable for units.

Const (Constant):
at the same elevation. Press the Shift key and left-click to the required elevation. Now hold down the left mouse button and level the terrain to the defined elevation.

Crack:
creates a subsidence.

Normalize:
levels the selected terrain.

 Normalize all

Brush Size:
changes the brush size.

Brush Force:
Strength of the brush.
