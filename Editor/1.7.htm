1.7 Bridges
 

A particular location may often be inaccessible. To reach a far shore, for example, you can build a bridge. Choose one of the available bridge types (in "Objects" then from the first list choose "Roads, bridges etc." and from the list below choose "Bridges".
From the list on the right you can choose one of the bridge types) and put it on the terrain. Using "+" "-" buttons you can increase and decrease the number of bridge elements (every bridge consists of several simple elements).

The easiest way to build bridges:

  • choose a bridge type,
  • put the required number of bridge elements on the flat terrain. (You can make the bridges as long as you wish),
  • choose "Flat" from the "Altitudes" tab,
  • hold down the "SHIFT" key and the left mouse button to lower the terrain under the bridge.
